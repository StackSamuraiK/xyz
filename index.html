Department Information

Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)

The Computer Science and Engineering department focuses on developing the skills necessary for software development, system design, and computer technology.

Information Technology (IT)

The Information Technology department emphasizes the use of technology in business and provides a strong foundation in computer applications and networking.

Electronics and Communication Engineering (ECE)

The Electronics and Communication Engineering department offers insights into electronic devices, communication systems, and signal processing.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE)

The Electrical and Electronics Engineering department prepares students to work with electrical systems, electronics, and power generation.
